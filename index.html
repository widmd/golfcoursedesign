Key Elements of a Strategic Golf Hole

A great hole challenges the golfer while rewarding skillful play. Here's how architects structure their work:

  • **Tee Box Placement**
    1. Forward (Red) Tees: Shorter play, easier angle.
    2. Middle (White) Tees: Standard amateur challenge.
    3. Back (Black) Tees: Championship length and difficulty.
  • **Fairway Contours** - Introducing elevation and doglegs.
  • **Hazard Placement** - Bunkers and water features designed to influence club selection.
  • **Green Complex** - Shaping and speed of the putting surface.
